WHY TECH MENTORS??

At Wheaton, you'll find:

World-Class Academics

Deepen your faith through intellectual testing as you work with premier faculty who are true mentors and guides.

Curiosity & Conviction

With a 10:1 student-faculty ratio, our 50+ majors and concentrations have an average class size of 23 students, allowing you to dive deep into the subjects you are passionate about.

Christ at the Core

Our one-of-a-kind core curriculum is marked by academic excellence and grounded in the person of Jesus Christ and his truth as revealed in the Scriptures.

Wisdom & Growth

Learn from top professors who are also today’s Christian thought leaders.

Hands-On & Collaborative Learning

At Wheaton, you’ll have exceptional opportunities at places like our Black Hills Science Station and specialized academic centers that focus on areas as diverse as applied Christian ethics, urban engagement, outdoor leadership, human needs and global resources, and faith and innovation.

Grace & Connection

Through campus houses, halls, clubs, choirs, and teams, you’ll form microcommunities that offer a place not only of growth, faith, and academics but of inspiring and generous fun—a safe, challenging, and profoundly real second family.

Total Excellence

At Wheaton, you don’t have to settle. With top rankings in U.S. News & World Report, Forbes, and Money magazine, as well as Kiplinger’s consistently rating Wheaton as one of the best values in higher education, you can confidently choose Wheaton.

Your Ambitious Calling

Here, you will find limitless ways to grow into your ambitious calling. Wheaties become movement makers, entrepreneurial shapers, and pathway breakers. We become scientists, lawyers, doctors, CEOs, filmmakers, engineers, policy experts, educators, renowned scholars, investment bankers, musicians, entrepreneurs, and, yes, church leaders, missionaries, and theologians.

Do you believe in finding your ambitious calling? At Tech Mentors, we believe it's possible. We believe you will find it here.
